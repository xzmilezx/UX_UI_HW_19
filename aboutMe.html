about me

I’m Tu Pham — a creative UX/UI designer

with a passion for arts, innovative and aesthetically

pleasing yet functional designs. Ever since I can remember, I have
been into arts and design. I enjoy drawing and painting, doing arts
and crafts, sewing, logo designing, and totally love coming up with
innovative solutions to problems big and small. I guess you can say
that I have been a designer at heart since forever. For me,
past year has been an intense but rewarding learning
experience on UX/UI design process and theory, design tools,

and psychology’s role in design. My journey has
been fueled by my insatiable desire to learn and

grow, hone my skills, and
apply my talents in the
field that I undeniably love.
